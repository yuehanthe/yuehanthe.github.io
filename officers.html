Meet the Officers

Hillary He

Founder/President

"I never thought I would be creating a club, but when I couldn't find the community I was looking for, I knew what I had to do.
Although I've always had a fascination with music, I only started playing clarinet second semester of 7th grade. From there, I struggled to find a place to create music outside of school band. That's why I created this club. It's a place for musicians of all levels to make connections with eachother and share music they love."

Miao Hong

Co-Founder/Vice President

"I am fluent bilingual that was a clarinet player in middle school. I played piano at the age of five, but failed miserably and don’t know how to play it anymore. In addition to the history of failed piano, I also have a history of failing violin, which I also don’t know how to play anymore either. Currently, I have a dream to become a percussionist."

Isha Goyal

Co-Founder/Vice President

"I have played instruments since I was young. Though I have skipped around the many different types of musical genres, I ultimately found my place within the classical area. Being the eldest sibling has taught me more than enough responsibility, but whats one more project before college?"

Rachel Heit

Teacher Sponsor

"I am the oldest of 4 siblings, I have 2 sisters and 1 brother. I also have a cat and a dog. I love listening to Latin music, going to concerts, trying new restaurants, and traveling. Some of my favorite places I have been to are Costa Rica and Spain."
