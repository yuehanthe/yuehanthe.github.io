About Us

Lambert Musical Arts Club is a music club based in Lambert High School.

We offer musician, as well as anyone else interested in music, the opportunity to play music in a fun and stress-free mannar. Classes and extracuriculars like marching band can be very stressful, as well as a big time commitment. Musical Arts club offers short term performance oppertunities as well as bi-weekly (every two week) jam nights.

Our mission

The club was founded after a student noticed a lack of open admission music clubs that offered a community of musicians engaging in all aspects of music. Frustrated by this, the student founded this club, creating a place for musicians to share their music as well as learn from each other.
